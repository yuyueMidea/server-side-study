**设计几组具有实际应用价值的主从表结构，涵盖不同业务场景：**

一、博客系统 - 文章与评论
- 主表: articles：  `id, title, content, author_id, category_id, status, view_count, created_at, updated_at`;
- 从表: comments:  `article_id, user_id, parent_comment_id, content, like_count, is_approved, created_at`;

二、在线教育 - 课程与章节
- 主表: courses: `id, title, description, instructor_id, price, duration, difficulty_level, enrollment_count, created_at`;
- 从表: chapters: `course_id, chapter_number, title, content_type, video_url, duration_minutes, is_free_preview, sort_order`;

三、库存管理 - 商品与库存记录:
- 主表: products: `id, sku, name, category_id, brand, unit_price, reorder_level, supplier_id, created_at`;
- 从表: inventory_transactions: `product_id, transaction_type, quantity, unit_cost, reference_number, operator_id, warehouse_id, created_at`;

四、项目管理 - 项目与任务:
- 主表: projects: `id, name, description, project_manager_id, client_id, budget, start_date, deadline, status, created_at`;
- 从表: tasks: `project_id, task_name, description, assignee_id, priority, estimated_hours, actual_hours, status, due_date, created_at`;

五、餐厅系统 - 菜单与配菜:
- 主表: menu_items:  `id, name, description, category, base_price, preparation_time, is_vegetarian, spice_level, created_at`;
- 从表: item_options: `menu_item_id, option_type, option_name, additional_price, is_required, sort_order, availability`;

六、医疗系统 - 病患与就诊记录：
- 主表: patients: `id, patient_number, name, gender, birth_date, phone, emergency_contact, insurance_info, registered_at`;
- 从表: medical_records: `patient_id, visit_date, doctor_id, symptoms, diagnosis, treatment, prescription, follow_up_date, record_type`;

七：房地产 - 楼盘与户型
- 主表: properties
- `id, property_name, address, developer, total_units, completion_date, average_price, amenities, created_at`
- 从表: unit_types:
- `property_id, type_name, bedrooms, bathrooms, area_sqm, price_per_sqm, floor_range, available_units, layout_image`
